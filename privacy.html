Netralis Inc. ("Netralis," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through netralis.ca and our products, and how we use it.

  • Contact information you provide voluntarily (e.g., your email address when you join our waitlist or contact us).

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with infrastructure providers (e.g., Google Cloud) strictly to operate the service, and only as required by law.

4. Your Rights
